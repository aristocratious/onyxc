DESIGN MATTERS

The design and functionality of a website in the 21st century is infinitely important for a great online presence.

Responsive websites optimized for sales

We only design responsive sites.  This means they look good and proportional on all devices.  Whether your customers are on their phones, tablets, or computers, the website will be functional and appealing.

Original and proprietary graphics

Our graphic designers create logos, banners, and more. Unlike some agencies that prepare generic logos and templates, we pride ourselves in our original works.

More than just drawing

Much strategy is put into a design. The various aspects of a design strategically-chosen include: the color scheme, composition, shape, width and height, symbolic meaning, literal meaning, psychological cues, and negative/positive connotations.

Scalability is key

In the ever-changing world of varying screen sizes and increasing resolutions, we have our designers work in vector format (as opposed to bitmap format). Vector format can be scaled to any size without losing quality. EPS and AI are the most common exported formats.
