E-COMMERCE, OPTIMIZED AND SIMPLIFIED

E-commerce websites can be optimized to make listing, selling, shipping, and getting paid for products easier. Aside from increasing sales, we focus on optimizing and horizontally integrating all associated tasks with online commerce.

Existing Platforms

We use existing, reputable e-commerce platforms to maintain security, reliability, and ease of use.

Sales Optimization

One of our specialties is increasing sales. A few ways (but not all of the ways) we increase sales on e-commerce websites:

  • Automated email receipts with recommendations for customers to purchase more
  • Highly-targeted advertising campaigns to your website visitors, frequent customers, and most likely to buy visitors.
  • Heat maps to determine where people are getting confused on the website (and then fixing these inefficiencies)
  • Requesting feedback from viewers to assist with confusion or problems

Fulfilling Optimization

Great, you’ve made an online sale. Next is receiving payment, processing the order, making a shipping label, shipping the product to the buyer, adding the tracking number to the website, and following up… Not so fast, we can automate many of these tasks so you can spend your extra time elsewhere.
